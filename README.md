## 👨‍💻 About Me

I am a BTech CSE student and developer from Kerala, India, passionate about solving real-world problems by blending clean frontend design, robust backends, and scalable cloud infrastructure. 

Beyond writing code, I am the founder of **OwnTheSite**—an initiative aimed at delivering high-performance business websites with **zero lock-in** and **no recurring subscriptions**.
